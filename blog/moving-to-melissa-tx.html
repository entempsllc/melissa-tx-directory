Complete Guide to Moving to Melissa, Texas

Melissa, Texas is one of the fastest-growing cities in North Texas. Here's what new residents need to know.

Why Melissa?

  • Excellent schools (Melissa ISD)
  • Family-friendly community
  • Affordable housing
  • Growing city with new amenities
  • Easy access to Dallas (about 40 minutes)

Schools

Melissa ISD serves the community with excellent schools, including Melissa High School, Melissa Middle School, and Melissa Elementary.

Housing

New housing developments are popping up across Melissa. The town offers more affordable options than nearby McKinney or Frisco.

Community

Melissa maintains a small-town feel despite rapid growth. Community events bring neighbors together throughout the year.
